Users access -

	adm (administrator)
		General access (including remove functions);

	fin (Financial)
		Access to Financial system (except remove functions);
		Access to Product functions (except remove functions);
		Access to Feedstock functions (except remove functions);

	man (Manager)
		Access to Product functions (except remove/confirmation functions);
		Access to Feedstock functions (except remove/confirmation functions);

	sto (stockist)
		Access to Feedstock storage (only storage access and confirmation functions);
		Access to Product storage (only storage access and confirmation functions);

	n/a (No Access);
